## Duplicate Analysis

### Same Authors Across Multiple Languages (Valid Entries)

These are NOT duplicates, but the same author appearing in different language sections:

1. **Ibn Kathir** - Appears in: Arabic Tafsir, English Tafsir, Urdu Tafsir, Bengali Tafsir, Turkish Translation
2. **Mufti Muhammad Taqi Usmani** - Appears in: Urdu Translation, Tafsir E Usmani
3. **Sayyid Abul A'la Maududi** - Appears in: Urdu Translation (Roman)
4. **Rwwad Centre for Translation** - Organization appearing in: Turkish, Moore, Croatian, Bisayan, Maguindanao, Vietnamese
5. **Al-Mukhtasar** - Appears in 25+ tafsir languages (same tafsir translated into multiple languages)

### Data Issues Identified

- **"Unknown" entries** standardized as "Unknown Translator"

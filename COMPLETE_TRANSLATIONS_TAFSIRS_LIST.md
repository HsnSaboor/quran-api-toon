## Topics Section
The repository includes a comprehensive **Topics** system that categorizes Quranic verses by subject matter. 
- **Available Languages:** Arabic, Bengali, German, English, Spanish, Persian, French, Hindi, Indonesian, Russian, Turkish, Urdu, and Chinese.
- **Data Structure:** Each topic provides a localized name and a collection of relevant verse references (Surah:Ayah).
- **Usage:** Ideal for subject-based search and thematic study of the Quran.
